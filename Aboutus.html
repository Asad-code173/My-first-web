Why MIC?

It is hard but its life changing!

Founded as approved institute by skill development council in october 2003,
The Mahars institue of computer is very popular institute across the pakistan
renowned for its Quality education,teaching staff and for infrastructure. we have
6 computer labs having 20 computers in each lab and 6 highly qualified teachers. Number of ourstudents are currently serving all around the world in software
houses, Multinational companies, educational institues. Our vision is to produce
students globally with innovative leadership capabilities So that they will
conribute in nation sucess.

We expect to develop students who are not only responsible citizens and empathetic
human-beings but are also successful in professional and entrepreneurial endeavors
that they seek. We focus on cultivating leaders who may come up and grasp every opportunity for assuming influential posts in various public and private sector
institutions in Pakistan and abroad. By producing highly knowledgeable, innovative
out-of-the-box thinkers and life-long learners who possess leadership skills, MIC
is determined to contribute towards bringing a positive change in the Pakistani society.

MIC always sets high standards when it comes to the quality of education. We define high quality education as one which produces students to compete with other students
for placements from some of the best computer programs offered by many institues in developed countries. Not only that, We guide those who wish to start their own successful entrepreneurial ventures. To ensure quality, MIC inducts highly talented
meritorious students and teachers to guarantee that it operates as a top-tier institution
in Pakistan in the selected disciplines that it pursues.
