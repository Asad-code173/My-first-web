Certified Graphics Designer – Karachi

INTRODUCTION

Graphic design is a craft where professionals create visual content to communicate messages. By applying visual hierarchy and page layout techniques,
designers use typography and pictures to meet users’ specific needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs, to optimize
the user experience.

10 Popular graphic design career opportunities

  1. Graphic designers develop graphics and layouts for product illustrations, company logos, Web interface and more.
  2. Creative director.
  3. User experience (UX) designer.
  4. User interface (UI) designer.
  5. Production artist.
  6. Product developer.
  7. Art director.
  8. Marketing Specialist.
  9. Multimedia Artist.
  10. Freelancer.
